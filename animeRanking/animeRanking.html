My Anime Ranking

  • 1

    Your Lie in April

    Really great and emotional series about a prodigy pianist, who ever since his mother's death has been unable to find the spark with the piano he once had. However, his life begins to change when he meets a violinst. Would really reccomend for those who like classical music or are just looking for a more thoughtful series.

  • 2

    Attack on Titan

    Absolute banger of an anime. Giant human-eating titans, slicing and dicing, intense soundtrack, actually good and coherent plot, and non-stop action. If you love the action genre or just want to feel adrenaline pumped right into your body, I reccomend this anime for sure.

  • 3

    Death Note

    Follows the story of Light Yagami, an A student who one days finds a mysterious notebook on the sidewalk. This notebook, known as the Death Note, is later revealed to have the power to kill people just by writing their name in it. The series follows Light's slow but unsettling unraveling as he explores the powers of the Death Note. Those who like the mystery or thriller genre will love this for sure.

  • 4

    Haikyuu

    An inspiring story about the journey of volleyball player Hinata Shoyo as he aims to become one of the world's best spikers, despite his rather short height. Must-watch for those who like sports shows or sports in general.

  • 5

    Violet Evergarden

    This show follows Violet, a child soldier who, having spent all her life learning and fighting in war, must learn how to live and find purpose in the war's aftermath. Another one of the more thoughtful shows that will make you reconsider your reality.

Other Recommendations

Demon Slayer

An action-packed anime about a boy named Tanjiro and his sister-turned-demon Nezuko. This show follows Tanjiro as he ventures into the demon-infested world in an effort to find the cure that will turn his sister back into a human.

Your Name

A Tokyo city boy and country girl suddenly find themselves switching bodies. Chaos and shenanigans ensue as they try to resolve their body-switching anamoly.
